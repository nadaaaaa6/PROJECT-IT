Project Name ( Stay safe)

A website to introduce the Corona virus, its transmission methods, associated symptoms, ways to prevent it, and some tips to prevent infection with it

This website is designed to provide comprehensive information about the Coronavirus, its transmission methods, associated symptoms, ways to prevent it, and tips to prevent infection with it. It will include detailed information about the virus, including its origin and spreadon the virus. The website will also provide resources for those who are seeking more information on the virus and its effects. Additionally, it will provide tips on how to protect oneself from infection and how to reduce the risk of spreading the virus. it will offer advice on what to do if one suspects they may have been infected with the virus.
